A Morphotype & Taxonomy Clearinghouse

Recipes for integrating Malesian plant biodiversity information

Building on a decade of botanical fieldwork in Indonesia, this ‘unproject’ addresses two serious concerns for users of taxonomic names for the plants of Malesia: “What can I do if I don’t know or don’t trust the available names for this plant at hand?” And “There’s much information on taxonomic names out there, but it is not integrated.” Tools to address these concerns include:

  • A platform for matching plant morphotypes using online images from multiple sites, and an RDF vocabulary for making federated statements about the plants’ morphological similarity (“i2i”).
  • Bespoke recipes for extracting and collating information about plant taxa from disparate websites, and resources for making statements about the likelihood that a plant is the same species as the type individual of a named species (“i2t”).
